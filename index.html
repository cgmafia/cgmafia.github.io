My skills grew from a technocrat to success leader who understood the gaps between business, products, services, and customers and solved them through the technological vision. While working in Sweden since 2018, I had the opportunity to work with various digital giants and develop solutions for them, while confidently delivering values delivering better products and services, raising the levels of customer experience, and retaining customers.
